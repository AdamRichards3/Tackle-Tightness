Exercise Name

Hamstring curls

Leg raises

Exercise Description

Lay on you back in a door way. Put your good leg through the door. Put you affected leg up on the wall, keeping your leg straight. hold the stretch for 1 minute and repeat 2 to 4 times

Lay on the floor with a pillow underneath you. From there rasie the affected hamstring up towards your buttock and lower it back down to floor. Repeat 8 to 12 times

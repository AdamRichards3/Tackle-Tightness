Introduction

This web application will be here to support rugby players with there road to recovery. It will give them exercises to do based on the injury they have and will give details on how to preform them.

What are we trying to achieve?

With the web application we are trying to reduce the amount of rugby players that do not follow all the advice given to them by a physiotherapist. We are hoping to reduce the channce of players retiering from rugby early
